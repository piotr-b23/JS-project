Printing service costs.

Printing as a service is a viable option for owning a printer in your company. You pay the initial price of the printer, and then pay monthly for pages you printed in black and color. The company you signed the deal with takes care of everything excluding paper. No need to worry about parts to fix the printer when it brakes.
